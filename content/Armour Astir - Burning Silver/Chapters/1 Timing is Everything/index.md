Years in the shadows, making plans, and striking covertly, the Resistance is finally ready to make their first major move. The plan is to strike out at a major resupply base operated by the [[The Argent Court#The Protection Guild]], which intelligence collected by a [[The Resistance#The Tears of the Great Ash Eye|Tears of the Eye]] shade suggests is currently preparing a new weapon to deploy against the [[The Resistance#The Free Duchies of Nald]].

In addition, a [[The Resistance|Quake#Quake]] cell raiding military supply vehicles nearby have acquired some crucial information, but were unfortunately picked up on a subsequent operation against [[The Argent Court#The Grand Unified Mining Concern|The Mining Concern's]] operation in the mining town of New Volt.

New Volt lurks some way behind the frontlines of the ongoing border war like a great black welt in the desert. A public execution of malcontents and enemies of the Empire is scheduled for noon the next day, the plan is for your team to get in and rescue the [[The Resistance|Quake#Quake]] team, then make use of their stolen information to infiltrate the military base and destroy or capture the new weaponry being prepared.
