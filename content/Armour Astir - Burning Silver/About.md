These are the setting notes and mission reports for a game of [Armour Astir](https://royalrabbit.itch.io/armour-astir) I am running.

This is a mythic age prequel for the game of [[Salvage Union - Ashkar/About|Salvage Union]] I ran over 2 years. [[The Argent Republic]] were an interesting element in the story, and I thought it would be fun to approach them more head-on in a game, and what better than fighting the good fight against them in a game like Armour Astir.

I didn't want to tie us too far down into "lore" and "canonicity" and "not having dragons in the game" so I've decided we're doing this as an explicit age-of-myth story. It is an allegorical tale about the history of the galaxy in the game of Salvage Union, and therefore we can (and will) have dragons in it.
